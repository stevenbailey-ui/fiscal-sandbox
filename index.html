How it works. Aggregate tax effects use HMRC ready-reckoner coefficients (£bn per 1p / 1ppt / £ change). Receipts grow with nominal GDP; non-interest spending grows with the inflation + real-spend assumptions; debt interest = your effective rate × prior-year debt; the deficit is added to debt each year. No behavioural, growth or investment feedback is modelled — these are deliberately excluded as unforecastable. Accurate for modest changes only.
