Model: Tax effects use HMRC ready-reckoner coefficients. Receipts scale with nominal GDP (real growth + inflation). Non-interest spending grows at inflation + real-spend assumption; capital investment grows with nominal GDP. Debt interest = blended rate × prior-year debt. No behavioural, growth or investment feedback. Current balance = receipts − (spending excl. capital investment). Overall balance drives debt. Fiscal rules: current budget surplus + debt/GDP falling in yr 5.
